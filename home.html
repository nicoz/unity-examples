First Demo

The first demo is simply a navigation-interaction showcase. The character is free to roam the island, and to interact with the objects that lay next to him.

First Inventory Demo

This demo starts to show the basic structure needed to form a propper inventory system. Feel free to press the "I" key (or to press the small "I" icon on the bottom left corner of the screen and try out the inventory. The inventory objects may or may not interact with the 3D objects on screen.

First Person Movement with a real body

This is a concept I would like to include in my games. I love First Person Shooters, but I really dislike not been able to see my own body (at least my arms and legs), so I made this little demo. Try moving around the scene using the classical movement keys (mouse movement is temporarily disabled.

This first part of my official first game
